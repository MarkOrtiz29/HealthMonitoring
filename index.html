About Us

Our app is specifically designed for low income individuals that need them the most. While other services will provide high fees and give you more questions than answers, we provide an digestable system in which can more easily monitor your health at a price that is next to zero.

Our group is composed of an eight group team who is working effortlessly in providing the following:
Links to doctor approved websites updated daily, a customized profile with graphs and charts to better understand what category your health falls into, and most of all the trust you've given us in helping improve your day to day life.
